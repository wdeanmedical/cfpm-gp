Select a topic from the left menu to get started

Add User

Follow the steps below to add a new user:

  1. While logged in as an administrator, select "Admin" in the Top Navigation Bar.
  2. Select the "User Admin" button.
  3. Select "Add User" in the lower right.
  4. Enter the name and contact information in the "Add User" form. Fields with asterisks are required fields. Note that assigning the user the Role of Admin will allow them to add users to the system or modify user information.
  5. Once you have entered all the user information, select "Submit" to add the user to the system.

Edit User

Follow the steps below to edit an existing user:

  1. While logged in as an administrator, select "Admin" in the Top Navigation Bar.
  2. Select the "User Admin" button.
  3. Find the user in the list in the User Manager and select "View Details."
  4. Make the desired changes in Edit User window.
  5. Select "Close.".

Logging In

Follow the steps below to log in:

  1. At the login screen, enter your assigned username and password
  2. Click the "SIGN IN" button.

Password Reset

Follow the steps below to reset your password:

  1. At the login screen, click on "Forgot Credentials?"
  2. The Credentials Recovery window will open. Enter the email address associated with your account in the field and click the "Submit" button.
  3. Check your email inbox for a message with the subject line "Password Recovery"
  4. Open the message and click on the contained link, as directed.
  5. In the Create Password window that opens, enter a new password in the Password field. (Acceptable passwords are at least 6 characters long, have at least one uppercase and one lowercase letter, at least one numeric character and one punctuation character.)
  6. Reenter your new password in the Confirm Password field.
  7. Click the "Submit" button.
  8. If the new password is acceptable and the two fields match, your password will be reset. A confirmation will appear at the top of the window.

Parking Your Session

Follow the steps below to park your session:

  1. In the upper-right select Park from the settings (gear) drowndown
  2. Now your session is parked your placed will be saved until you log back in
  3. Enter your username and password in the Park dialog and select "Sign Back In' to sign in
  4. Alternatively, select "Log Out" to log out of your session.

Printing

Follow the steps below to print:

  1. In addition to any print button on a given screen there is also the main Print button in the top menu bar.
  2. Upon pressing this button, if there is a printable screen currently in view, it will bring up the print dialog.

Create Patient

Follow the steps below to create a patient:

  1. Click on the Patient button in the top menu bar. If already in the Patient section, Click on the Change Patient button in the top blue contenxtual menu.
  2. Click on the green New Patient button at the top of the Patient Search form.
  3. Fill out at least the required fields in the form.
  4. If the patient has a guarding click on the "has guardian" check box and add a new guardian. You may also choose an existing guardian.
  5. All available Intake Forms are pre-selected. You can unselect any of the the forms that should not be sent to this patient at the time of Portal Invitation.
  6. By default the Patient is placed in the Wait List. Switch status to Active if that is desired.
  7. Click the Save button to save the New Patient. You are then taken to the Patient Search form the new patient is now listed in the Recent Patients list. Select that patient in the list and you will be taken the the new patient's chart.

Intake Forms

Follow the steps below to work with patient intake forms:

  1. Intake forms are selected at the time of Patient creation or in the Patient Info form if the patient was already created without sending forms.
  2. Once sent to the patient to fill out in the Patient Portal, the forms also appear in the Patient Intake section of the Patient Chart.
  3. At this point the forms can be edited within the Patient Intake section and the Patient Portal concurrently.
  4. Once the forms are completed and submitted, they become read-only.

Update Demographics

Follow the steps below to update patient demographics:

  1. In the Patient Chart click on the Patient Info button
  2. The View/Edit Patient form will appear. Here you can update any of the patient information.
  3. The option to delete the paitent is presented with a red Delete button at the bottom of the form.

Patient Forms

Follow the steps below to work with patient forms:

  1. In the Patient Chart click on the Patient Forms button
  2. In the Forms screen the full history of Patient Forms are listed. Here you can click on a form listed in the table and view it.
  3. The available forms for assignment are listed in the drop-down next the the Assign Form button
  4. To assign a new form to a patient choose a form and click the Assign Form button.
  5. The newly assigned form with the "assigned" status is now listed in the assigned forms list
  6. As the patient views and eventually submits the form the status of the form will update.
  7. To view any form, click on the form in the assigned forms list and it will appear below.

Patient Files

Follow the steps below to work with patient files:

  1. In the Patient Chart click on the Patient Files button
  2. In the Patient Files screen the files sent from Provider are listed by default
  3. To send a new file to the patient click on the Add File button. Select a file by clicking on the Choose a file... button and enter a description of the file. Click the Add button and the file is now available for the patient to download from the Patient Portal.
  4. Additionally there is a Files To Provider left tab that when clicked, display all files sent by the patient to the provider
  5. Any files can be downloaded or deleted by the provider.

Patient Letters

Follow the steps below to work with patient letters:

  1. In the Patient Chart click on the Patient Letters button
  2. In the Letters all previously issued letters are displayed in the list.
  3. All available letters to send are displayed in the dropdown to the right of the New Letter button.
  4. To compose a new letter select a letter from the dropdown and click on the New Letter button. The letter template appears below.
  5. Make all needed edits to the letter.
  6. The newly created letter can be saved for later sending by clicking the Save button, sent by clicking the Send button or deleted by clicking the Cancel button
  7. A letter that has been saved can be clicked on in the table and worked on further. At any time while it has not yet been sent it can be sent or cancelled.
  8. When the letter is sent it appears in the inbox in the patient's Portal and a notification of communication email is sent to the patient.

Creating an Appointment

Follow the steps below to schedule an appointment:

  1. Click on the Schedule button in the Top Navigation Bar
  2. Click or click-drag on the calendar to bring up the New Appointment dialog.
  3. The new appointment will default to one hour at the point of click unless there was a click-drag specifying a different duration.
  4. The Clinician field will auto-complete as you type.
  5. Clicking the Add button will add the appointment and notify the patient of the upcoming appointment in the form of a message.

Modifying an Appointment

Follow the steps below to modify an appointment:

  1. Click on the Schedule button in the Top Navigation Bar
  2. An appointment can be moved by dragging it to a different calendar spot.
  3. Clicking on an appointment brings up the Edit Appointment dialog.

Encounter Form

Follow the steps below to work with patient encounter forms:

  1. In the Patient Chart click on the Encounters button.
  2. All previously Encounters appear in the list.
  3. If you have an open Encounter, the form it will automatically open.
  4. Otherwise, to open a new Encounter click the New Encounter button
  5. All updates to the Demographics section are reflected in the Patient Info form found in the Patient Chart.
  6. All updates to the Medical History section are reflected in the Medical History form found in the Patient Chart.
  7. All updates to the Family History section are reflected in the Family History form found in the Patient Chart.
  8. Each section of the Encounter form will also be visible in as its own form, accessible in the patient chart once the Encounter is completed.
  9. To complete an Encounter click on the Complete Encounter button at the top of the form.

Message Folders

Follow the steps below to work with message folders:

  2. In the Messages screen the messages in the Inbox are listed by default.
  3. Sent contains your sent messages.
  4. Draft contains messages you have saved but not yet sent.

Sending a Message

Follow the steps below to send a message:

  1. To compose a new message click on the Compose New button
  2. Recipients are autocompleted as you enter them.
  3. If the notify checkbox is checked then the recipents in that field will receive an email notification that there is a new message.

Available Reports

Follow the steps below to work with the reporting capabilities:

  1. Click on the Reports button in the Top Navigation Bar
  2. In the Reports screen the available reports are listed.
    • Activity Log: View all user activity.
    • Clinician Activity: View all Clinician activity.
    • Patient Activity: View all Patient activity.
    • Wait List: View all Patients on the Wait List.

Exporting to CSV

Follow the steps below to export a report:

  1. After a report is run click on the Export button
  2. If you have an application installed on your system that can view the export, you will be presented with an open/save system dialog box, otherwise it will just prompt you to save the file.
  3. At this point you can view or save the exported file.

Billing Ticket

Follow the steps below to work with billing tickets:

  1. In the Patient Chart click on the Billing Ticket button.
  2. Billing ticket entries, most recent first, appear in the list.
  3. A new Billing Entry is created by clicking the New Entry button.
  4. The Billing Entries can be edited at any time by editing a field in the entry.

Invoicing

Follow the steps below to create a patient invoice:

  1. In the Patient Chart click on the Patient Invoicing button.
  2. All previously created invoices appear in the list.
  3. An invoice can be accessed by clicking on one in the list.
  4. A previously created invoice that is not yet sent may be edited.
  5. A new invoice is created by clicking on the New Invoice button below the list.
  6. Invoice items can be added by clicking on the Add Item button
  7. Once an invoice is sent, its status changed to 'sent'.
    Once viewed by the patient in the portal it becomes 'viewed'.
    The status of paid Invoices are set to 'paid'.
